# Transparent Man

A 2D platformer adventure where a completely colorless man must venture into a dangerous world to win back his true colors.

---

## 📖 Story

In a vibrant world, the Transparent Man has lost his color. To become whole again, he must traverse the treacherous Meadow, fighting off bizarre creatures and overcoming deadly obstacles to reclaim what is rightfully his.

---

## 🎮 Core Mechanics

As the Transparent Man, you rely on your agility and strength to survive:

* **Platforming:** Run and jump through the dynamic terrain of the Meadow.
* **Combat:** Strike back! Use your punching abilities to hit enemies and clear your path forward.

---

## 🌍 The World: The Meadow

The entire game takes place in **The Meadow**, a seemingly peaceful green landscape that is actually crawling with hostile creatures standing between you and your lost colors.

### 👾 Enemies

* **Rollons:** Dangerous purple spheres that patrol the area. Direct contact with a Rollon will damage the player, so you must carefully dodge or attack them.
* **Bigas (The Boss):** The ultimate challenge of the Meadow. Bigas is a highly aggressive and relentless boss. He attacks violently, using high jumps and fierce charging rams in a brutal attempt to destroy the Transparent Man. You will need perfect timing to take him down!
